The Best-Selling Fiction Authors
of All Time

The chart to your left shows every fiction writer with reported sales at least 100 million books. Hover over the bubbles to learn more.

A few caveats: I pulled this data from a wikipedia page on the topic, as part of a D3 visualization project in my advanced interactive multimedia class at UNC. Book sales are notoriously difficult to estimate, and well, wikipedia. Several writers are likely missing from the chart because their publishers have not publicly released their sales figures.

Finally, please don't get your undies in a bunch about the genres. Should Lewis Carroll be in Children/Young Adult, or "proper" Literature? Since the answer is "both," I chose the genre based on which shelves you'd probably find the books at the local bookstore.
